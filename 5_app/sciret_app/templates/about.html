About this project

SciRet

Compute-Aware Multimodal RAG for Reproducible Scientific Question Answering

The original idea

SciRet started with a simple observation: during the COVID-19 pandemic, important scientific findings were published faster than most people could read them. Clinicians, journalists, and curious members of the public had no reliable way to search across thousands of papers and get a plain-language answer — with sources they could verify.

The original project was called "SciRet: Make Scientific Research Available for General Public." That mission drives every design decision — from the choice of open-source models to the compute-aware architecture that runs on a laptop.
